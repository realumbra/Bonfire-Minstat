The Bonfire Minstat Guidelines

~ The complete and detailed ruleset for Minstat runs in The Bonfire ~

When the community talks about 'minstat runs' they are discussing runs in which the player intentionally starts the game with the minstat equivalent class then completes the game without leveling up. For Souls this is Soul Level 1 (SL1), for Bloodborne it's Blood Level 4 (BL4) and for Elden Ring it's Rune Level 1 (RL1). (Sekiro has it's own unique system and it's Base Vit/Posture)

These runs have a long history in the community, which spawned a multitude of unique challenges. In this page you can read general info on Minstat Runs as well as criteria for the proof you need to submit in the Bonfire Discord Community.

Global Guidelines

    • No other class shall be used. These are the only classes in the games that start at Minstat, and as such these must be the classes selected by the player.
    In Demon's Souls you will need to pick Royalty. This class starts at SL1.
    In Dark Souls I you will need to pick Pyromancer. This class starts at SL1.

    In Dark Souls III you will need to pick Deprived. This class starts at SL1.
    In Bloodborne you will need to pick Waste of Skin. This class starts at BL4.
    In Elden Ring you will need to pick Wretch. This class starts at RL1.

  • Submissions

    • Each game has it's own unique criteria regarding run proof, please refer to the individual games section to understand what you will need when submitting proof for your run!

  • Submitting your run

    • When submitting your run for checking we usually require screenshots of your stats and boss souls, bonfires or boss arenas. It can vary depending on the game. When you believe you have all of the necessary proof send Aspen#2352 a private message on Discord.

  • All bosses must be slain!

    • Every boss listed under the game must be slain. A boss can be defined by the sight of a health bar and/or a fog gate (regardless of the need to enter through such) but exceptions may be made. All required bosses for each individual game are listed in their dedicated section.

    Make sure to fully read the section you're interested in doing before starting your run!

  • No exploits will be tolerated

    • There's multiple cheese tactics or exploits in these games. Any level skips, glitches that break boss AI or glitches that change the fight to what it's not intended as are not tolerated. Exceptions may be made on a case-by-case basis. It comes down to the discretion of the person checking your run. None are allowed. By cheating you are undervaluing yourself and others runs.

    Important! In terms of "glitches", it is generally allowed to dupe or spawn in items as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character. Items may also be spawned in if the runner accidentally missed the opportunity to obtain them and can no longer do so. An example would be Dragonslayer's Axe in Dark Souls III which can be missed entirely through a questline.

    Please let the checker know if you spawned in or duped any items.

  • No help allowed.

    • Killing bosses via summoning players or with the help of NPCs is not acceptable. Summoning players to help you progress through the game is also not allowed. Every run found breaching this rule will be disqualified.

    Important! In terms of trading, it is generally allowed to get items dropped as long as it simply cuts down farming time and the items can be farmed by the character. Items may also be traded to you if the runner accidentally missed the opportunity to obtain them and can no longer do so, usually because of questlines.

  • No cheating.

    • Multiple "interesting tactics" have been already been tried and subsequently cut down. The entire point of a challenge run is to challenge yourself. These directly combat that. Each run will be looked at closely, so don't try anything.

    It is not allowed to obtain items before it is usually possible. No debug. No scripts nor external tools.

    - Turning on iframes
    - Script-killing bosses via flags
    - Messing with stats
    - Cheesing the AI...


    ... and so on will result in a disqualification of the run in question, with possible further moderation action to be taken.
